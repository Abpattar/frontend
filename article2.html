Why Color and Contrast Matter

Color plays a key role in how users perceive and interact with your website. It helps guide attention, express mood, and create visual harmony. Good contrast ensures that text, buttons, and other elements stand out clearly, making your site easier to read and more accessible for everyone.

Choosing the Right Color Palette

A balanced color palette helps maintain consistency across your website. Use one dominant color for your brand identity, one or two accent colors for highlights, and a few neutral tones for backgrounds or text areas. Avoid using too many bright or clashing colors ‚Äî simplicity often makes designs feel more professional and calm.

Creating Accessible Contrast

Contrast is the difference between two colors, such as text and background. High contrast improves readability, especially for users with visual impairments. Always make sure body text contrasts strongly with its background ‚Äî for example, dark text on a light background or vice versa. You can use online tools to check contrast ratios and ensure they meet accessibility standards.
